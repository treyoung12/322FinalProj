Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sneak Peak?

Sneak Peak is a place where sneaker heads, hypebeasts, and other fellow shoe fanatics can come and educated themselves on when shoes are dropping, where they are dropping, and how much they cost. We value ourselves in giving as much correct information as possible when trying to help others with release updates. We also sell and help verify shoes that are 100% Authentic if you do not know what to look for.


How do I know if my shoes are fake?

If you email us directly , we will verify the shoes and get back to you within 24 hours of us receiving the email. Visit our shop to find our list of verified sites/sellers.


Do you have a social media or an app?

Currently we do not have a social media, but we are in the process of creating social outlets for the customers to follow. If you have any questions or concerns regarding Sneak Peak, please email us.


Do you buy shoes?

No we do not buy or trade shoes on our site.
